Welcome to ChartTracker!

If you enjoy music, then this site is for you. Here you can view music chart data from all around the world of music. Wondering about the current charts? We've got them here. Been curious what the top ten songs were during the week you were born? You can find it here.

This site is still being updated regularly. There are many features we look forward to adding such as artist charts, artists' chart history performance, and many other chart-related options. We hope to allow for user-customized charts in the near future. And one exciting feature we're currently researching is the ability to generate playlists for the major music sites using chart data found here.

So take a look around. Use the menu above to find the charts you're looking for. Take a look at our custom-tabulated year-end and decade charts. If you have any questions, shoot them our way. We look forward to hearing from you.
